How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same rank touch, they merge into one! The ranks used in this game are: 拖延-熬夜-构思-草图-一草-建模-深化-通宵-二草-正图，不同的时期挂掉会有不同的结局。
